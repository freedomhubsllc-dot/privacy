# AURA DAILY COMPANION - PRIVACY POLICY
**Last Updated: June 2026**

### 1. INFORMATION COLLECTION
Aura Daily Companion (operated by Freedom Hubs LLC) collects mobile phone numbers and caregiver contact information solely to provide real-time senior safety alerts, automated daily cognitive tracking updates, and emergency notification routing. 

### 2. PURPOSE OF TEXT MESSAGING
By providing your phone number and opting into the Aura Daily Companion service, you consent to receive recurring automated operational text messages (SMS) relating to senior safety status, check-in alerts, and emergency communications. Message frequency varies based on user settings and system alerts. Message and data rates may apply.

### 3. SMS SAFE HARBOR & DATA PROTECTION
Freedom Hubs LLC values your privacy. Mobile numbers collected for the purpose of SMS opt-in, automated tracking, and safety alerting will NEVER be shared, sold, rented, or leased to third parties, affiliates, or marketing entities for promotional or commercial purposes under any circumstances. 

### 4. OPT-OUT & HELP
You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just text "STOP" to our phone number. After you send the SMS message "STOP" to us, we will send you an SMS message to confirm that you have been unsubscribed. To get help, text "HELP" to our number or contact support at our official channels.# privacy
